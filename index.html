Biography

I have been an assistant professor in School of Computer Science and Technology at Harbin Institute of Technolgy, Shenzhen since Fall 2021. Before that, I received my PhD degree at University of Macau (2021), advised by Prof. Derek F. Wong and was a member of NLP2CT Lab. I was a postgraduate intern at Tsinghua NLP Group (2018), advised by Prof. Yang Liu and a research intern at Tencent AI Lab (2019-2021), advised by Dr. Zhaopeng Tu and Dr. Longyue Wang. My current research interests focus on deep learning for natural language processing (NLP), e.g., machine translation and grammar error correction.
