Biography

I have been a master student in School of Computer Science and Technology at Beijing Institute of Technology, Beijing since Fall 2020, advised by Prof Heyan Huang and Prof Xianling Mao. Before that, I received my undergraduate degree at ZhengZhou University (2020), advised by Prof. Hongying Zan. I was an engineer intern at WechatAI, Beijing, advised by Yong Hu and a research intern at Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen, advised by Prof. Xuebo Liu. My current research interests focus on automatic writing, especially for scientific, e.g., grammatical error correction and automatic essay scoring.
I am looking forward to receiving a Ph.D. offer and hope my Ph.D. life works on helping researchers write.
